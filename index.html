Charly Mithrush Art



Painting a Story

I have always loved the way art can create feelings inside us, it can be thought provoking, or we can purely enjoy the vision and journey of the artist. When I start out painting I have an idea or a story in mind, but this can change at anytime when I am carried along on the path of creativity. I get lost in the process, no longer aware of my surrounding, It leads me off, I follow, and the story evolves. Creating is my meditation, and I am at peace, painting, music playing and letting go. The flood gates open and the real journey begins. A favourite quote by Pablo Picasso rings true to the artist in all of us....

"The artist is a receptacle for emotions that come from all over the place: from the sky, from the earth, from a scrap of paper, from a passing shape, from a spider’s web. "

About Charly Mithrush - CSV


Charly Mithrush is a contemporary artist working predominantly in acrylic, mixed media and encaustics. She has attended Emily Carr as well as a variety of college level programs, and continues to hone and learn new skills through attending and giving private workshops. Her works consist of bold colours, objects and textures on various surfaces and she is presently exploring encaustics. Charly primarily works in her home studio on the Sunshine Coast in B.C., Canada. Her artwork has been featured in over 20 group and solo shows, and in Artists of British Columbia Volume 3.
